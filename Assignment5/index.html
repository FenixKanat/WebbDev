About Authors:

Astrid Lindgren:

Astrid Lindgren was a Swedish writer who is best known for her children's book series featuring the character Pippi Longstocking.

William Shakespeare:

William Shakespeare was an English playwright, poet, and actor who is widely regarded as one of the greatest writers in the English language and the world's preeminent dramatist.

J.K. Rowling:

J.K. Rowling is a British author, philanthropist, and screenwriter who is best known for writing the Harry Potter fantasy series.
